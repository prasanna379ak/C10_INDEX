C_INDEX-10

Rule-based crypto market index · Informational only

Index Overview

C_INDEX-10 measures the performance of the largest cryptocurrencies by market capitalization using a fixed, transparent methodology. It is designed to reflect market behavior, not predict it.

Methodology (v1)

• 10 assets total
• Bitcoin fixed at 30% weight
• Market-cap ranking for selection
• Stablecoins & derivatives excluded
• Quarterly rebalancing
• Market-cap normalized index

No financial advice. Index values are indicative and may be volatile.
